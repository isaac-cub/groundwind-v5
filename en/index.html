About us

An investment company built to create lasting value

Ground Wind is a Spanish investment company focused on businesses operating within the fitness, wellness, health, transformational education and healthy lifestyle sectors.

The company operates as a highly selective professional investment platform with a long-term commitment to building sustainable value through a portfolio of high-quality equity investments.

Its operational headquarters in Palma de Mallorca provide a stable, international and strategically positioned base aligned with a modern European business identity.

Founder

Jeffrey Perlman Founder
The world's most valuable brands don't simply sell products; they change behaviours, build communities and improve people's lives.

Ground Wind was founded by Jeffrey Perlman, an international brand strategist and business operator with a distinctive career across the global wellbeing industry.

His public track record includes serving as former Chief Marketing Officer of Zumba and Chief Strategy Officer of Mindvalley — two leadership roles that reflect a rare combination of brand strategy, product vision and international scaling expertise.

That experience represents one of Ground Wind's greatest competitive advantages: practical knowledge of how globally relevant companies are built, scaled and positioned.
