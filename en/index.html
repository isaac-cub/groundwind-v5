Who we are

An investment company built to create lasting value

Ground Wind is a Spanish company specialized in investing in businesses within the fitness, wellness, health, transformational education and healthy-lifestyle universe.

The company is structured as a professional, highly selective investment platform with a long-term outlook, focused on building value over time through a portfolio of quality holdings.

Mallorca provides a stable, international location consistent with a contemporary, European corporate identity.

Founder

Jeffrey Perlman Founder
The most valuable brands don't just sell a product; they change behavior, create community and elevate people's lives.

Ground Wind is founded by Jeffrey Perlman, a brand strategist and operator with a singular international track record in the wellness ecosystem.

His public experience includes his tenure as former CMO of Zumba and his role as Chief Strategy Officer of Mindvalley — two experiences that reflect an uncommon combination of brand vision, product sensitivity and international expansion capability.

That experience is one of the company's key differentiating assets: a practical understanding of how companies capable of building global relevance are born, grow and position themselves.
